How To Use

Add Expense

Tap + Add Expense, enter the details, and save.

Track Monthly Salary

Tap the + Salary button to calculate your remaining balance automatically.

Edit or Delete

Tap ⋯ to edit expenses, or use the 🗑️ icon beside a month to delete it entirely.

Backup Data

Use the Export and Import icons at the top right to backup or restore data.
